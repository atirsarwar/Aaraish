Welcome to Aaraish

Aaraish is our innovative eCommerce platform designed to blend the elegance of high-end fashion with luxurious home furniture. Inspired by the sophistication of brands like Gul Ahmed, this platform aims to redefine online shopping by integrating style, convenience, and cutting-edge technology under one cohesive digital roof.

“Bringing luxury and comfort to every home — powered by creativity and technology.”

Project Highlights & Features

  • Elegant UI/UX: Designed with user-friendly interfaces and smooth navigation, emphasizing luxury aesthetics.
  • Responsive Design: Fully optimized for devices of all sizes, from smartphones to desktops.
  • Secure Authentication: Robust signup/login system with data protection and encrypted credentials.
  • Product Management: Dynamic catalog updates, categorized by fashion and furniture products, with smooth filtering and search options.
  • Modern Tech Stack: Frontend with React.js, CSS3, and JavaScript; Backend using Node.js and Express.js; Database management with MongoDB.
  • Creativity Tools: UI/UX designed using Figma and Canva for polished, professional visuals.
  • Performance & Scalability: Backend architecture optimized for performance and future growth potential.
